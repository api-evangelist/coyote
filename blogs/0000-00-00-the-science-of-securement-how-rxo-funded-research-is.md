RXO funded research at Clemson University examining how dunnage airbags behave under extreme altitude and temperature conditions. The study reveals differences in how Kraft paper, woven polypropylene, and PVC materials perform during mountain passes, providing data-driven insights to reduce cargo damage and improve load securement across the freight industry.
